Our Services

Chameleon Specialty Chemicals also offers contract or toll manufacture activities at its production site in Lianyungang. We currently toll manufacture ranges of adhesives and of lubricants and our toll manufacture customers include Dow Chemical and Tricool international.
Chameleon is particularly well suited to product ranges that require careful in process control and which have high final product quality requirements. Our background in imaging chemical manufacture means that we regularly produce products with high purity and are have established strong quality control testing capabilities. Our toll manufacture customers also value Chameleon’s responsible attitude and high environmental and safety standards as well as, of course, our very cost competitive location and manufacture setup. We have both ISO 9000 and 14000 accreditation.

The plant setup is flexible and enables manufacture of a wide range of products in either small or large volumes. We can offer production in our extensive range of existing equipment or investment in bespoke and dedicated equipment depending on our customer’s requirements.

Chameleon’s site infrastructure offers both 3 bar steam and hot oil heating systems, cooling water and chilled (sub zero) cooling, nitrogen and both vacuum and pressure. Our reactors, heat exchangers and filters enable us to handle solvents and both acidic and alkali reaction conditions. We also have a range of liquid and solid packaging and milling options.

Chameleon has a very experienced team and our depth of knowledge and pro-active attitude enables us to work directly with customers to find solutions to their manufacturing requirements.
