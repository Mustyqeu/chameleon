Welcome to Chameleon

Speciality Chemicals was established in 2008 and by late 2009 had completed construction of its production plant near Lianyungang on China's east coast. The plant commenced manufacture in early 2010 and our business has been growing ever since.
company has been founded and managed by a team with extensive experience of the imaging chemicals business. Hence Chameleon Speciality Chemicals has been established as a highly focused, well managed operation using the best process technology in a custom designed and built plant. Our location, technology, expertise and production plant setup all combine to make Chameleon a highly efficient and competitive supplier of imaging chemicals.
