Meet The Team

Chameleon Speciality Chemicals has established a very strong and committed management team with extensive experience of the imaging chemicals business.
Geraint Davies BSc FCA
Managing Director

Geraint has almost 20 years of broad commercial experience in the paper chemicals and imaging chemicals business including 4 years as General Manager of Ciba's Imaging Chemicals business. Geraint is responsible for overall business management and particularly for business development.

Email: geraint.davies@chameleonsc.com
Tony Huo BSc MBA
Production Director and General Manager for China

Tony has broad experience within the chemical industry in China and has particular expertise in EHS and the manufacture of imaging chemicals. He has worked for 15 years with multinational companies in the pharmaceutical and fine chemical sectors including 4 years as Deputy General Manager of Ciba's colorformer joint venture in Shougang, China. Tony is responsible for the management of Chameleon's production facility in Lianyungang, China and also for domestic sales in the Chinese market.

Email: tony.huo@chameleonsc.com
Ian Hill ASDC C.Col
Technical Director

Ian is a leading expert in colorformer production and manufacturing processes with over 20 years experience including 9 years as Head of Process Development and Technical Support for Ciba's Imaging Chemicals business. Ian's responsibilities remain in the areas of process development and production support with a particular emphasis on productivity, EHS improvements and new product research and plant introduction.

Email: ian.hill@chameleonsc.com
Roy Kirk
Development Director

Roy has many years experience in the area of Research and Development of Imaging Chemicals including over 10 years working for Ciba's Imaging Chemicals business. Roy specialises in process development and new product introductions as well as providing production support when needed.

Email: roy.kirk@chameleonsc.com
Jeff Sewell BSc
Engineering Director

Jeff has 30 years engineering experience in a variety of leading chemical and manufacturing organisations including 13 years as Engineering Manager of Ciba's colorformer manufacturing plant. Jeff provides expert design and guidance during the construction and subsequent expansions of Chameleon's manufacturing site at Lianyungang in China.

Email: jeff.sewell@chameleonsc.com
